Welcome to Observation!

By Observation | Documentary | May 21, 2024

Observation is a 100% open-sourced site to watch videos for free! No subscriptions or anything no ads or whatever, just a website to watch videos for completely free without ads! Observation is in beta, and this is a Observation 'Document'. Documents in Observation are not videos, but it's like a podcast without voice or sounds. Just like Google Docs, if you've ever used it before.
Uploading videos/documents will come soon! Observation/OV is still in its alpha stages.
